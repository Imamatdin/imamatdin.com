## Overview

This website serves as a digital notebook, inspired by Leonardo da Vinci's codices. It showcases research proposals, projects, reading lists, and writing.

## Technical Stack

- Next.js 13 with TypeScript
- Chakra UI for styling
- MDX for content management
- Vercel for deployment
