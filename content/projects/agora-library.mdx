## Overview

Agora Library is a Telegram-based knowledge sharing platform that makes books and learning resources accessible to communities. Built with a focus on simplicity and ease of use.

## Features

- Book cataloging and search
- Resource sharing between communities
- Reading list management
- Integration with external book databases
