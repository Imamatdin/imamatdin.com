A few simple but true principles on what it takes to take an already good company and turn it into a consistently great company, backed by a data-driven study on real companies. Conceptually, the complement to _Zero to One_. The strategies are all quite simple and intuitive, but certaintly true. Mostly a set of tips for managing a company well.
